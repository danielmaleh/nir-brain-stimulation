Topology: logic-level MOSFET low-side switch per channel. Each load's + goes to its supply +, its − to the MOSFET output, the MOSFET GND to the common ground bus, and the Arduino pin (D9 NIR / D10 heater) drives the gate. The NIR strip runs at 24 V; put the heater on its own rail and confirm its rated voltage before applying 24 V. Every ground symbol and the bus are the same node. Functional checks use firmware/io_test + io_test.html (Chrome, one page connected at a time).
