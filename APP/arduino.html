Hardware Specifications

  • DS18B20 1-Wire temperature sensor resolution is set to 10-bit.
  • Safety Cutoff activates instantly if temperature sensor reading meets or exceeds 40.0 °C.
  • Target heater temperature is 37.5 °C with a 0.5 °C hysteresis window.
  • LED Blinding constraint: Mode LEDs turn off completely while active stimulation is running.
