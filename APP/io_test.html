ℹ️ STBY = the TB6612's master enable. On this build it is tied to 5V, so the driver is always on and the Y toggle does nothing (the board replies "NA"). It only becomes a real control in the optional Arduino-controlled variant (TB_STBY_CONTROL=1, Pin 12). Do not leave the heater on indefinitely.
